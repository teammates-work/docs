---
title: Glossary
description: 'Understand Our Terminology'
---

- **Teammate**: a virtual colleague, also known as an autonomous AI agent, that collaborates with you and your team, chats in Slack and other channels, plans work, operates SaaS tools, and more.
- **Assignment**: a task given to your teammate. The teammate will understand your objectives, plan the it out, and begin working
- **Skill**: an ability given to a teammate such as reading documents, performing online research, or generating images.
- **Tool**: one of _your_ digital software tools that your teammate can operate, such as Salesforce or Slack
- **Integration**: the setup and authorization of your tools to the Teammates platform. You first authenticate with Teammates, then give each individual teammate access, as desired.
- **Rules**: a collection of when-this-then-that logic that teammates use when events like a new email arrives, a deal closes, or a new document is created.
- **Handbook**: background information, policies, and procedures that teammates use to plan and complete their work.
- **Squad**: a group of Teammates that can work together on complex, multistage tasks
