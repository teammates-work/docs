## Setup a Teammate

Learn how to configure your first teammate and put them to work

### Design your teammate

- Give them a Form like a robot, alien, turkey, or your favorite animal. This part is just for fun!
- Give them a Role like Marketing Manager or Research Assistant. 
- Name them
- Give them a job description e.g. You help plan our social media campaigns.

### Chat with them to say hi

> What can you do?

### Give them their first assignment

Let's start with something simple like "What day is today"
